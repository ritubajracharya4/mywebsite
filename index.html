I am a lecturer in Computer Engineering at Kathmandu Engineering College, affiliated with Tribhuvan University. I completed my M.Sc. in Computer System and Knowledge Engineering in 2023 with an aggregate of 89.27%, building on my B.E. in Computer Engineering from the same institution.

My teaching covers core engineering subjects — C Programming, Operating Systems, Object-Oriented Programming, and Object-Oriented Analysis and Design — alongside practical lab instruction in Linux, C++, and UML tools.

Beyond the classroom, I'm drawn to the intersection of optimisation algorithms and distributed systems. My thesis explored task scheduling in cloud environments using Quantum Annealing — a problem I find endlessly fascinating. I believe the best engineers are those who never stop building.
